## KEY TAKEAWAYS

### Where You Excel (Competitive Advantages)
1. **AI Hub** — Most comprehensive AI suite in real estate CRM: content generation, voice calling, configurable lead scoring, cost monitoring, BYOK, per-user settings
2. **Campaign Intelligence** — A/B testing with stats, ML send-time optimization, AI phone campaigns — nobody else has this
3. **Workflow Builder** — Visual drag-and-drop with execution history, auto-retry, webhook triggers
4. **Enterprise Admin** — Immutable audit trail, feature flags, health checks, API key management
5. **Security** — 2FA, session management, login history with device/IP/geo tracking
6. **Reporting Depth** — Custom report builder, scheduled exports, multi-touch attribution

### Where Competitors Excel (Your Gaps)
1. **No IDX Website / MLS Integration** — This is your #1 gap. kvCORE, CINC, Lofty, and Sierra all bundle IDX websites with live MLS data. This is often the first thing agents look for.
2. **No Built-in Ad Management** — CINC manages $30M+ in ads for clients. Lofty and Sierra also offer done-for-you Facebook/Google ad campaigns.
3. **No Lead Generation Engine** — Competitors generate leads for their users. You manage leads but don't help capture them.
4. **No Power Dialer** — Follow Up Boss and Lofty have polished built-in dialers. Your call logging exists but lacks a dedicated dialer.
5. **No Video Email/Texting** — A niche feature, but LionDesk popularized it and some agents expect it.
6. **No Mobile App** — kvCORE, CINC, and Lofty all have dedicated mobile apps.
7. **Pre-built Smart Plans** — Competitors offer "set it and forget it" behavioral automations. Your builder is more powerful but requires more setup.

### Features You Share (Parity)
- Full lead lifecycle management with pipelines and stages
- Email + SMS multi-channel campaigns
- Drip campaign automation
- Role-based team management with permissions
- Lead scoring and segmentation
- Analytics dashboards with conversion tracking
- Template libraries for emails and SMS
- Stripe-based subscription billing
- Webhook and API integrations
- CAN-SPAM compliance and unsubscribe management

## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### High Priority (close the biggest gaps)
1. **Add IDX Website Integration** — Partner with an IDX provider or build MLS integration. This is table stakes for real estate CRM buyers.
2. **Build a Lead Generation Module** — Landing pages, home valuation pages, and squeeze pages would make you competitive with the all-in-one platforms.
3. **Develop a Mobile App** — Agents live on their phones. A dedicated app is expected.

### Medium Priority (strengthen your position)
4. **Add Facebook/Google Ad Integration** — Even basic ad campaign management would close a major gap.
5. **Build a Power Dialer** — Leverage your existing Twilio integration to add sequential/parallel dialing.
6. **Create Pre-built Smart Plan Templates** — Reduce the setup time for your workflow automation with ready-made templates.

### Low Priority (nice-to-have differentiators)
7. **Video Email/Texting** — Integrate with BombBomb or build native video messaging.
8. **CMA (Comparative Market Analysis) Tools** — Lofty's Dynamic CMA is popular with listing agents.
